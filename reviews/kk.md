```
        <source>Right-click to edit address or label</source>
        <translation>Мекенжай немесе белгі өңдеу үшін оң клик</translation>
    
YES  
The translation is incomplete and somewhat unnatural. The original English says "Right-click to edit address or label," which instructs the user to perform a right-click action in order to edit the address or label. The current translation literally says "Address or label edit for right click," missing the verb form that clearly conveys the action "to edit" with a right-click.

A more accurate and natural translation would be:  
"Мекенжайды немесе белгіні өңдеу үшін оң жақ түймешігін басыңыз"  
This means "Press the right button (right-click) to edit the address or label."
```

```
        <source>Sign messages with your Bitcoin addresses to prove you own them</source>
        <translation>Хатқа Bitcoin мекенжайларын қосып, олар сізге тиесілі екенін дәлелдеу</translation>
    
YES  
The translation is incorrect and incomplete in Kazakh. It mistranslates "Sign messages with your Bitcoin addresses to prove you own them" as "Add Bitcoin addresses to the message to prove they belong to you," which changes the meaning and omits the action of signing messages. A correct translation should convey signing the messages with your Bitcoin addresses to prove ownership.

Correct translation:  
Хабарламаларды Bitcoin мекенжайларыңызбен қол қойып, олардың сізге тиесілі екенін дәлелдеңіз
```

```
        <source>Verify messages to ensure they were signed with specified Bitcoin addresses</source>
        <translation>Хат тексеріп, берілген Bitcoin мекенжайлары қосылғанына көз жеткізу</translation>
    
YES  
The translation has some grammatical and stylistic issues in Kazakh. The phrase "қосылғанына" literally means "added" or "connected," which is incorrect in this context where the intended meaning is "signed." The word "signed" should be translated as "қол қойылған". Also, the phrase could be made clearer and more natural by adjusting the sentence structure.

Correct translation:  
Хаттарды тексеріп, олар берілген Bitcoin мекенжайларымен қол қойылғанына көз жеткізу
```

```
        <source>Request payments (generates QR codes and bitcoin: URIs)</source>
        <translation>Төлем талап ету (QR кодтары мен биткоин құрады: URI)</translation>
    
YES  
The translation is somewhat inaccurate and awkward. The English phrase "generates QR codes and bitcoin: URIs" means that the system creates QR codes and bitcoin URIs, but the Kazakh translation "QR кодтары мен биткоин құрады: URI" literally reads as "creates QR codes and bitcoin: URI," which is ungrammatical and confusing due to word order and punctuation.

A better translation would preserve the meaning clearly and use correct grammar and punctuation, for example:

"Төлем талап ету (QR кодтарын және bitcoin: URI-ларды жасайды)"

This means: "Request payments (generates QR codes and bitcoin: URIs)".  

Note:
- "QR кодтарын" is the correct partitive plural form.
- "bitcoin: URI-ларды" keeps the colon as in the source and pluralizes URI appropriately.
- "жасағышты" can also be used for "generates," but "жасайды" is standard and appropriate.
```
