        <translation>Khethani ḓiresi ine na ḓo ṱanganedza ngayo tshelede ya tsimbi nga .</translation>
    
ERR
There is an issue with the translation:

1. The phrase "ya tsimbi" literally means "of metal" and is not a correct translation for "coins" in the context of Bitcoin (where "coins" refers to digital coins, not literal metallic coins). 
2. The sentence ends awkwardly with "nga .", which is grammatically incorrect and incomplete.
3. The translation should not include a period at the end if the source does not (to prevent stray punctuation).

Correct translation:
Khethani ḓiresi ine na ḓo ṱanganedza ngayo zwikoini.

Explanation:
- "Zwikoini" is commonly used for "coins" in relation to digital currency in Tshivenda, rather than the literal sense "tshelede ya tsimbi".
- The sentence is now complete and concise, matching the intent and structure of the source.
